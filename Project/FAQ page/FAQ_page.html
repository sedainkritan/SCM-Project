Frequently Asked Questions

What is Swap & Share?
Swap & Share is a community platform where people can exchange items they no longer need for something useful, saving money and reducing waste.
How does swapping work?
You list an item you want to give away, browse items from others, and agree on an exchange. Once both parties confirm, you arrange delivery or pickup.
Is there a fee to use the platform?
No, Swap & Share is free to use. We don’t charge listing or transaction fees.
What types of items are allowed?
You can list everyday items like books, clothing, electronics, furniture, and more. Prohibited items include illegal goods, hazardous materials, or anything against our community guidelines.
Is my information safe?
Yes. We protect your data and only share necessary details with other users during confirmed exchanges.
How do I contact another user?
You can send a message through the platform’s built‑in chat once you’re interested in an item.
Can I swap across categories (for example, books for electronics)?
Yes, you can exchange items across different categories as long as both parties agree.
Do you offer delivery or is it self‑pickup?
Delivery or pickup is arranged directly between users. Swap & Share does not handle shipping.
How do I report inappropriate content or users?
Click the “Report” button on the item or profile. Our team will review and take action if necessary.
